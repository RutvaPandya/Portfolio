About Company

Fivestar Dehydration Private Limited, est. 2002, is India's premier exporter of dehydrated onion, garlic & other veggies. Known for quality, eco-friendly practices & innovation.

 

About Fivestar Dehydration Private Limited

Fivestar Dehydration Private Limited is a well-established dehydrated manufacturing company with a state-of-the-art production capacity of over 5000 MT annually. The company was founded in 2002 by Mr. Nihal G. Bhurani, a respected and renowned figure in the growth of the dehydration industry in India.

The company prides itself on delivering high-quality products to its customers, and has gained a reputation for excellence in the industry. Fivestarâ€™s products are widely exported to over 40 countries, including Australia, the United States, Canada, Spain, Brazil, Italy and many others.

Fivestar Dehydration P. Ltd. is committed to providing its customers with the best possible service and the highest-quality products. The company has been audited and credited by major international organizations, including BRC, Kosher, FSSM 220000, APEDA and Halal, which is a testament to its commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Apart from this Fivestar is also the member of Star Export House Recognised by the government of India & is also the member of Gujarat Chamber of Commerce.

Fivestar Dehydration P. Ltd. offers a wide range of products that cater to various industries, including food, ready-to-eat, food chains, Restaurants, Etc. Its product range includes dehydrated onion, garlic, vegetables, fruits, herbs, spices, and other food ingredients. The company uses the latest technology and equipment to ensure that its products are of the highest quality and meet the requirements of its customers.

Overall, Fivestar Dehydration P. Ltd. is a highly reputable company in the dehydration industry that has built a solid foundation of quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

About Fivestar Group

The Fivestar Group is a diversified business conglomerate with primary interests in the dehydrated food industry through its subsidiary, Fivestar Dehydration P. Ltd. In addition to its dehydrated food business, the Fivestar Group also operates in other food-related businesses, such as manufacturing & exports of peanut butter, peanuts, vegetable Paste, Etc.

Apart from its food-related businesses, the Fivestar Group is also diversified into real estate and construction. The company has a proven track record of successful real estate development projects and construction ventures, and it continues to explore new opportunities in these areas.

Overall, the Fivestar Group is a dynamic and forward-thinking business conglomerate that is committed to delivering high-quality products and services to its customers. Through its various business units, the company is well-positioned to capitalize on emerging opportunities and drive sustainable growth for the benefit of all stakeholders.
