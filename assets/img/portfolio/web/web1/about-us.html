Company Profile

S.S.Industries (SSI) is manufacturer of Reinforcement steel bar in 6 mm to 32 mm sizes.The unit is based at Sihor, a hub of steel rolling mills, in Gujrat. Today company is a prominent player in the market.

Our strength is early anticipation and faster response.It is an inherent strength that gives us the time and space to innovate, to think laterally and to make the most of the technical opportunities.

Committed team of professionals run our enterprise who believes that evolution and revolution is the edifice on which their accomplishment stands.We uses latest technology to manufacture TMT bars and structural steel in with minimum energy use per ton and help to make india GREENER. The manufacturing time per unit is flattering time of 18 seconds against 52 seconds of others.The manufacturing plant is automated for hot rolling.
